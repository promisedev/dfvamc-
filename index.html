SCHOOL VISSION

To inculcate good morals into student with the aim of making them to be honest, truthful, respectful, have integrity, and to be able to show love to others.

Raising Godly children, who are to be confidient in themselves, desciplined, have a sense of humility, patience, kindness and forgiveness for one another.

To make students to be sound academically, teaching them to be problem solvers and not help seakers.
